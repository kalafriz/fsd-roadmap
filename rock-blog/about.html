About the Blog

Welcome to Boston Rockhound! I'm Nathan, and I'm thrilled to embark on this geological journey with you. As a passionate geologist based in the bustling city of Boston, I've always been captivated by the stories embedded in rocks and minerals. Boston Rockhound is more than just a blog—it's a celebration of the Earth's rich tapestry, woven with layers of history, chemistry, and awe-inspiring formations. From the towering granite outcrops of the Blue Hills Reservation to the tranquil shores of Cape Cod, New England is a playground for rock enthusiasts like us. Through engaging yet academic exploration, we'll peel back the layers of geological time, uncovering the secrets of ancient oceans, mighty mountains, and the forces that shape our landscapes. Whether you're a seasoned rockhound or a curious newcomer, there's something here for everyone. Join me as we embark on expeditions, share fascinating discoveries, and delve into the depths of Earth's crust. Together, let's uncover the hidden gems of Boston and beyond, one rock at a time. Welcome to Boston Rockhound—where every stone tells a story.
